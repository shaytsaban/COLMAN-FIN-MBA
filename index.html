üë®‚Äçüè´ About the Lecturer

Dr. Shay Tsaban, Ph.D. is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) and Senior Lecturer at Peres Academic Center. He holds a Ph.D. from Ariel University, where his research focused on Corporate Happiness Responsibility (CHR) and the economics of happiness in business contexts. Dr. Tsaban's academic background bridges accounting, finance, and behavioral economics, with research exploring how financial policies and corporate practices influence organizational success and stakeholder well-being.

With extensive professional experience in accounting and finance, Dr. Tsaban has served as a member of the Examination Committee of the Board of Accountants at the Ministry of Justice. His research has been published in academic journals and presented at international conferences, addressing topics such as corporate investment decisions, valuation, and the intersection of finance with social responsibility.

Dr. Tsaban's teaching philosophy emphasizes practical problem-solving and real-world application of financial principles, making complex concepts accessible and relevant for future managers and business leaders.
